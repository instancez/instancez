# Code of conduct

## The short version

Be decent to each other. This project is here for people who want to build things, learn, and help out. Harassment, personal attacks, and bad-faith behavior are not welcome, and we will act on them.

## What we expect

- Treat people with respect, including when you disagree with them.
- Assume good intent, and give the kind of feedback you would want to receive.
- Keep criticism about the code and the ideas, not the person.
- Respect that people have different experience levels, backgrounds, and time zones.

## What is not okay

- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Insults, demeaning comments, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome advances.
- Publishing someone's private information without their permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, or reviews.

## If something goes wrong

If you see or experience behavior that breaks these rules, report it to the maintainers.

Reports are handled privately. We will look into what happened, talk to the people involved, and decide on a response. That can range from a warning to a permanent ban, depending on how serious it is. We will keep the reporter's identity confidential.

## Scope

This applies anywhere the project lives: issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other space where you are taking part as a member of the community.
